Rooms & Suites

With a history of delighting guests and providing personalised service since 1834, the Grand Hotel Excelsior Vittoria is truly a Sorrento icon. Thanks to the recent refurbishment of some of the rooms, the reimagining of Sorrento’s first Grand Hotel is ever evolving.
81 elegantly appointed rooms and suites evoke a welcoming, residential ambience, while offering the conveniences of today.

Suites
