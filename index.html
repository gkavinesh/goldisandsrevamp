EXPERIENCES

The Goldi Sands Hotel, Four Star luxury hotel in Negombo nestled on the serene Negombo Beach,
Within this coveted tourist destination, guests are beckoned to immerse themselves in a world where relaxation meets adventure. Start your journey by sinking your toes into the powdery sands, where endless stretches of shoreline invite leisurely strolls and serene sunbathing sessions. For those craving excitement, an array of water sports awaits, from exhilarating jet skiing to mesmerizing snorkeling explorations amidst vibrant coral reefs. Delve into Negombo's rich cultural fabric with visits to the storied Dutch Fort, a testament to the city's colonial past, and the bustling fish market, where the day's freshest catches are auctioned amid a bustling atmosphere of local life. Nature enthusiasts can embark on tranquil boat rides through the verdant Muthurajawela Marsh, a haven teeming with diverse flora and fauna. As the sun sets, the hotel comes alive with vibrant entertainment and gourmet dining experiences, promising unforgettable evenings by the sea. Whether seeking blissful relaxation or exhilarating adventures, Goldi Sands Hotel offers a gateway to a world of tropical splendor and unforgettable moments along the shores of Negombo Beach.




This Negombo Coast Four Star Luxury Hotel is situated in a beautiful location on the shores of Negombo beach, with breathtaking view of the Bay of the Indian Ocean.
